- TripFormData is defined in types/database.ts as `Omit<TripInsert, 'user_id'>`

- TripFormModal lives in components/ (top-level), not in app/checkin/components/

- Next.js App Router page.tsx prohibits extra named exports — extract helpers to separate files (e.g., app/lib/) for testability
